Where Luxury Meets the Arabian Sea

Nestled along the pristine shores of Gorai Beach, Cuba Boutique Resort offers an unparalleled escape from the ordinary. Our exclusive 8-room sanctuary combines sophisticated elegance with the raw beauty of the Arabian Sea.

Each spacious premium room opens to breathtaking ocean views, while our sprawling garden and infinity pool create a private oasis where time stands still.
